# Brief: home

## Facts

- East Community Cross Country (ECXC): free, volunteer-run summer training group, Anchorage.
- For high school cross-country runners and nordic skiers; any high school athlete welcome.
- Eligibility: rising 9th graders through 12th grade, plus graduates home from college.
- No skiing background needed.
- Meets Monday, Wednesday, Friday mornings, 10:30 AM, June 1 to August 19, 2026.
- Mondays at East High; other days move among Anchorage trailheads (posted in CrewLAB).
- Activities: running, strength, mountain biking, roller-skiing.
- Four-day Talkeetna camp, July 21 to 24.
- Sign-up and waiver in CrewLAB.
