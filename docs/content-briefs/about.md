# Brief: about

## Facts

- East Community Cross Country (ECXC): free, volunteer-run summer training group for Anchorage
  high school cross-country runners and nordic skiers.
- Founded 2026 by Amy Purevsuren and Geoff Wright.
- "East" = East Anchorage, where the program is based. Not affiliated with the Anchorage School
  District, East High School, or any school, club, or business.
- Open to athletes from anywhere in the area: public school, private school, homeschool.
- Context, and the why-frame's actual logic (corrected 2026-06-09 after a Geoff flag): school
  teams cover the racing seasons (XC running in fall, skiing in winter), so summer is
  unstructured for any athlete not in a club. APU Nordic and Alaska Winter Stars train
  year-round INCLUDING all summer, 5+ days a week; the gap ECXC fills is affordability, never
  seasonality. Do not write anything implying clubs leave summer uncovered. ECXC does not
  replace those clubs and does not match their level of support.
- Summer program: June 1 to August 19, 2026, Monday/Wednesday/Friday mornings. Running and
  strength are the weekly core; mountain biking and roller-skiing mix in. Locations: East High,
  Hillside trails, Kincaid Park, Bartlett, Chugach front range. Independent workouts posted for
  off days.
- Talkeetna camp: July 21 to 24, 2026, cabins on a lake, training twice a day.
- Eligibility: rising 9th graders through 12th grade, plus graduates home from college
  (encouraged into volunteer team leadership). JN/AWG-track athletes train alongside first-time
  athletes; three training groups by experience and pace; adult volunteers ride/run with both the
  faster and slower ends.
- Cost: free. Donations optional and need-blind, used for gas, campground fees, group equipment,
  and gear or transportation for athletes who need it. Help with camp, gear, or rides available,
  no questions about family finances.
- What we ask of athletes: commit to training; give back through community service (trail work,
  helping at community events and races); take care of sleep, food, and school.
- Risks: falls, collisions, equipment failure, wildlife encounters; volunteers' care does not
  remove them. Signed liability waiver required before the first session, signed in CrewLAB;
  guardian signs for under-18, adults sign for themselves.
- Volunteers: unpaid; parents, former racers, local athletes. Volunteers page lists them.
